# Homepage Page Map

## 1. Header

Fixed header with AIV-web identity, primary navigation, and CTA. Header can hide while scrolling down and reappear when scrolling up.

## 2. Hero

Large first screen with strong typography, concise positioning, CTA pair, and performance/business-value proof points.

## 3. Scroll-reveal positioning text

Large text block where lines or phrases become active as the user scrolls. Used to explain the AIV-web approach: speed, structure, SEO, maintainability, growth.

## 4. Services

Four service areas: site development, SEO promotion, contextual advertising, support and growth.

## 5. Process

Four-step project flow: briefing and structure, design and prototype, clean CMS development, launch and growth.

## 6. Pricing preview

Compact preview of pricing/packages or starting points. Should guide users toward a consultation without overcommitting to universal prices.

## 7. FAQ

Six key questions about cost, timing, platform, content editing, support, SEO and ads.

## 8. Final CTA

Clear closing prompt to discuss a project, with a short value-oriented message.

## 9. Footer

Contact details, navigation, legal links, and minimal brand close.
